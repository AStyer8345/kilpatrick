The Long View

26 years of Austin. One agent.

Crystal arrived in Austin in 1993, worked her way through college as an apartment locator and property manager, and earned her Texas real estate license in 2000. She started with Keller Williams Realty in Southwest Austin and has been there ever since.

Twenty-six years in. CRS-certified — a designation held by fewer than 4% of Realtors nationwide, and one Crystal serves as Board Programs Chair for. Listings in Belterra, Bear Creek, South Austin, Hyde Park, and Barton Creek. Buyers placed in Kyle, Buda, Cedar Park, Comfort, and Lakeway. A price range that runs from a starter home in San Marcos to a multimillion-dollar Barton Creek estate — both closed in the same recent stretch.

Crystal is a member of Austin Luxury Network and Broker Underground, two invitation-only networks that surface select pre-MLS opportunities to active agents before they reach the open market. For buyers in Austin's tightest price points and for sellers who want their listing handled discreetly, that access matters.

Her team keeps the work clean. Amy Goldberg runs transaction coordination — the contracts, deadlines, vendor coordination, and document trail behind every closing. Kelly Gosey represents buyers and sellers alongside Crystal and brings a background in home design and remodeling that pays off when clients are weighing renovations or evaluating a fixer.

Outside the work, Crystal is an active runner around Lady Bird Lake, a longtime supporter of Austin Angels, Austin Pets Alive, and the Junior League of Austin, and shares her home with her family and rescue dogs.
