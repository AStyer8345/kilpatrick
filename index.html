Crystal is a member of the Austin Luxury Network, Broker Underground, and the Dripping Springs Elite — invitation-only professional networks among Austin and Hays County's most active agents. Through these networks, she can surface select pre-MLS and network-only opportunities before they appear on Zillow or the open MLS.
