Use this as a starting frame, not a verdict.

The numbers above are research baselines as of April 2026 — sourced from Realtor.com, Redfin, HAR, GreatSchools, Niche, and a few local market reports. They're directional. If two markets are close on this dashboard, that's almost always where the conversation should start: same broad fit, very different lived experience. That's where I add the most value.
